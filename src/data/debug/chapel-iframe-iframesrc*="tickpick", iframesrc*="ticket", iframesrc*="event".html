About

Chapel of Bones (located in Raleigh, NC USA) offers a variety of coffee, cocktails, wine, craft beer, live metal shows, burlesque events, independent films, parties, and more.

Chapel of Bones is esthetically inspired by the 16th century architecture of "Capela dos Ossos", a chapel of human skulls and bones built by monks as a reminder of the fragilty of life and mortality, in Evora, Portugal.
